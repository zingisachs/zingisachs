Our History

Zingisa School aims at being an institution of service excellence that provides quality and holistic education based on Christian values of Love, Care, Selflessness and Dedication.

Founded:

Founded in 1988 and commenced teaching in 1989, Zingisa Comprehensive High School was built on the idea of the Zingisa seminary, opened in 1972. It was converted by Fr Urs Fischer CMM with the help of the Catholic Bishops of the Xhosa Region(PCXR) in 1988. The school remains the property of the PCXR and will be administered by All Saints Parish, Umtata. The school, registered as Zingisa Comprehensive High School with the Transkei Education Department(Registration number: 702640-1), and will be run according to the Constitution of Zingisa Comprehensive High School.

Website Development

In 2022, a pair of grade 11 students Nkebe Sipho and Nongcula Milisa in partnership with LUSA Studio, made it their concern to develop a platform that would last forever in our community. They created this website. Nkebe Sipho undertaking all of the designing and development of the project as he is a self-taught developer, while Nongcula Milisa worked enthusiastically on the content writing of this project as she is an amazing and creative writer of all styles, including poems among many. This project will continue to be appreciated and will continue to help us make our way to the digital world of computers.
