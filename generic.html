About Us

Zingisa School aims at being an institution of service excellence that provides quality and holistic education based on Christian values of Love, Care, Selflessness and Dedication.

Our Mission

    We are called and we have accepted to provide among others:

  • a caring, Christian school community.
  • a Catholic school ethos.
  • holistic Education
  • a climate of respect for the dignity, rights and responsibilities of each person and group among us
  • special concern for the disadvantaged among and around us
  • effective administration and willing accountability at all levels
